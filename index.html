Can you provide information about campus housing and accommodation options?
At our college, we offer a variety of housing and accommodation options to cater to the needs of our students. Currently, we have three hostels - one for girls and two for boys. Additionally, we are in the process of constructing a new hostel to accommodate even more students. Our existing hostels provide comfortable living spaces equipped with essential amenities, including furnished rooms, communal areas, laundry facilities, and 24/7 security. The girls' hostel and one of the boys' hostels are located within the campus for easy access to academic buildings and other facilities. The second boys' hostel is situated outside the campus premises, in the Federal School campus. While it is not directly on campus, it offers convenient access to academic resources, and transportation arrangements are made for students residing there.
Please check our Housing and Accommodation page for more detailed information about room types, rules and regulations, and the application process. Our goal is to provide a safe and welcoming environment for all students, whether they are on campus or in our off-campus facilities.

How do I apply for admission to your college?
For our BTech undergraduate programs, admission is based on JEE Main scores. To get started, make sure you have appeared for the JEE Main examination. Visit our official website and access the online application portal to select your desired BTech program and complete the application. Alongside personal and academic details, you'll need to submit essential documents, including your JEE Main scorecards, and pay the application fee as per the fee structure provided. The application can be tracked through your application portal, and if selected, you will receive an acceptance letter with enrollment instructions.
For our PhD programs, please refer to the "PhD Admissions" section on our website. Here, you will find program-specific details regarding the application process, eligibility criteria, and required documents. Note that admission requirements may vary depending on the program and your student category. For comprehensive and up-to-date information, visit the Admission section on our website and don't hesitate to reach out to our Admissions Office for guidance during the application process. We're here to assist you in pursuing your academic aspirations with us.

What programs and majors do you offer?
We offer a range of academic programs tailored to meet diverse educational interests. In our BTech (Bachelor of Technology) category, we provide two specialized branches: Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) and Artificial Intelligence & Data Science (AI & DS). For doctoral (PhD) candidates, our offerings include three distinct research areas: Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AI & ML), and Mathematics and Computing. These programs provide an opportunity for in-depth research and specialization within their respective domains.
If you require further details about specific majors, courses, or program structures, we encourage you to explore our official website or contact our academic advisors for comprehensive information on available programs and their respective curricula. We're committed to supporting your academic journey and helping you find the program that aligns with your educational and career goals.
